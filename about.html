Connecting Communities, One Town at a Time.

TownBoat is the ultimate local hub, designed to bring neighbors, businesses, and communities closer together.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower local communities by providing a platform where residents can easily share information, support local businesses, and have a voice in their town's future. We believe that a strong community starts with strong connections.

What TownBoat Offers

Real-time Community Feed

Share what's happening in your neighborhood, ask for recommendations, or post updates. Connect with your neighbors and stay in the loop.

Exclusive Local Deals

Discover and share amazing deals and discounts from local businesses. Save money while supporting your town's economy.

Interactive Polls

Vote on important local issues, share your opinion on community events, or settle a friendly debate with your neighbors.

Business Directory

Find and review local shops, restaurants, and services. A curated directory to help you find what you need, right in your town.

Town Leaderboards

Recognize and celebrate the most active and helpful community members, businesses, and singers through our unique leaderboards.

Connect in Clubs

Join or create clubs based on your interests. From book clubs to hiking groups, find your people and share your passions.
