TownBoat: The Local Superapp

TownBoat is a hyperlocal platform designed to connect residents and businesses within a community. It's evolving into a one-stop destination for local interaction and services, empowering communities and supporting the local economy.

Sign Up to Continue Browse as Guest

Key Features and Functions

Real-time Community Feed

Engage with your neighbors by sharing updates, asking for recommendations, and participating in lively local discussions. Never miss out on what's happening in your town.

Exclusive Local Deals

Discover and share exclusive discounts and promotions from your favorite local businesses. It's a win-win: save money while supporting your local economy.

Interactive Polls

Create and participate in polls on local issues, upcoming community events, or friendly debates. Your opinion helps shape the conversation in your town.

Business Directory

A curated list of local shops, restaurants, and services. Find what you need, read trusted reviews from neighbors, and support local entrepreneurs.

Town Leaderboards

Get recognized for your contributions! Our leaderboard celebrates the most active and helpful community members and businesses, fostering a friendly spirit of engagement.

Local Clubs

Join or create interest-based groups to connect with like-minded people. From hiking clubs to book clubs, find your tribe right in your neighborhood.

Free Auto and Taxi Services

Simplify your local travel with our new function that allows you to directly call local auto drivers and taxis at no cost, right from the app.

Earn With Us: The Social Affiliate Program

To further engage the community, TownBoat is launching an affiliate program designed for influential social media users.

Eligibility

Must have at least 1,000 followers on Instagram or Facebook.

Affiliate Benefits

Promote TownBoat and local businesses to earn rewards for your contributions.
